Founder & Managing Partner

Katherina Lucero

Abogada (Attorney-at-Law)

Katherina Lucero is the founder and managing partner of Lucero Legal, where she leads the firm's immigration and international client practice. With a focus on providing clear, efficient, and results-oriented legal services, she has helped hundreds of clients from over 30 countries successfully navigate Argentina's legal system.

Her practice encompasses the full spectrum of immigration services, from initial visa applications to citizenship proceedings, as well as real estate transactions and corporate formation for foreign investors. She is particularly recognized for her ability to explain complex legal processes in accessible terms and for her responsiveness to client needs across time zones.

Prior to founding Lucero Legal, Katherina gained experience in civil and commercial litigation at leading Buenos Aires firms, developing the technical foundation and procedural expertise that informs her current practice.
