Immigration & Visa Services

Your bridge to legal residency in Argentina. We simplify the bureaucratic maze of visas, work permits, and documentation processing.

Overview

Argentina offers several pathways for foreign nationals to establish legal residency. The right option depends on your nationality, income sources, professional situation, and long-term goals. Our immigration practice covers the complete spectrum of visa categories and residency permits.

Visa Categories We Handle

Rentista Visa (Independent Income)

For individuals with documented passive income from abroad. Ideal for remote workers, freelancers, and those living on investments or savings.

Requirements: Proof of stable monthly income (typically equivalent to 30x minimum wage), apostilled documentation, background check.

Pensionado Visa (Retiree)

For retirees receiving a government or private pension. One of the most straightforward paths to residency for those of retirement age.

Requirements: Proof of regular pension payments, apostilled documentation, background check.

Investor Visa (Inversionista)

For those making a productive investment in Argentina, such as starting a business, purchasing commercial property, or investing in local enterprises.

Requirements: Business plan or investment documentation, proof of capital origin, legal fund transfer records.

Work Visa (Empleado)

For those with a job offer from an Argentine employer. Requires sponsorship from a registered local company.

Requirements: Employment contract, employer registration, professional qualifications.

MERCOSUR Residency

Streamlined process for citizens of MERCOSUR member and associate states (Brazil, Paraguay, Uruguay, Chile, Bolivia, Peru, Colombia, Ecuador).

Requirements: Valid passport, background check from country of origin, simplified documentation.

Family Reunification

For spouses, parents, and children of Argentine citizens or permanent residents.

Requirements: Proof of family relationship, sponsor's documentation, background check.

DNI & Documentation Processing

The DNI (Documento Nacional de Identidad) is Argentina's national identity document. Once you have approved residency, obtaining your DNI is essential for opening bank accounts, signing contracts, and accessing many services.

Our DNI services include:

  • Scheduling appointments at RENAPER (often weeks in advance)
  • Document preparation and verification
  • Accompaniment to the appointment (if needed)
  • Follow-up on DNI delivery
  • CUIT/CUIL tax ID registration
