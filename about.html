Our Story

Lucero Legal was founded in Buenos Aires with a clear purpose: to bridge the gap between Argentina's complex legal system and the expectations of international clients who deserve clarity, efficiency, and results.

Our founder, Katherina Lucero, recognized early in her career that foreign investors and expatriates faced a unique set of challenges in Argentina. Traditional law firms, while technically competent, often failed to communicate effectively across cultural and language barriers. Bureaucratic processes that locals navigate instinctively became insurmountable obstacles for newcomers.

We built this firm specifically to solve that problem. Every system, every communication, and every process at Lucero Legal has been designed with the international client in mind. We don't just translate documents—we translate the entire Argentine legal experience into something comprehensible and manageable.

Who We Serve

Our clients come from over 30 countries and share a common goal: to establish their lives, businesses, or investments in Argentina with confidence.

Expatriates & Relocators

Professionals joining the growing community of digital nomads, remote workers, and retirees choosing Argentina for its quality of life and cost of living.

Investors & Entrepreneurs

Business owners establishing operations, purchasing real estate, or exploring investment opportunities in Argentina's evolving economy.

Families & Spouses

Individuals married to Argentine citizens or with Argentine heritage seeking citizenship or permanent residency for themselves and their families.

Companies & Corporations

International businesses establishing local presence, from startup formation to multinational compliance requirements.

Areas of Focus

By maintaining a focused practice, we develop deep expertise rather than superficial familiarity. Our core areas include:

Immigration & Visa Services

All visa categories including tourist extensions, rentista, pensionado, investor, and work permits. DNI processing and Migraciones representation.

Citizenship & Naturalization

Argentine citizenship through residency, marriage, or descent. Complete judicial file management and court representation.

Real Estate Transactions

Property purchase and sale for foreign nationals. Title verification, Escribano coordination, and international fund transfers.

Corporate & Commercial Law

Business formation (SA, SAS, SRL), banking compliance, contract drafting, and commercial dispute resolution.
